Classic Margherita Pizza

A simple Italian classic with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, basil leaves, and olive oil. It is light, balanced, and perfect for a quick homemade meal.

Prep time 20 min
Cook time 15 min
Serves 4
Italian Pizza Dinner
Difficulty Easy
Cuisine Italian
Calories 300 per serving
Rating 4.6 / 5

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. 1Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. 2Roll out the pizza dough and spread tomato sauce evenly.
  3. 3Top with fresh mozzarella and basil leaves.
  4. 4Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  5. 5Bake for 12 to 15 minutes until the crust is golden brown.
  6. 6Slice and serve hot.
